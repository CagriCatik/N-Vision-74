# 3D Printing Checklist

## Printability

- [ ] All exported parts are solid bodies.
- [ ] Minimum wall thickness is acceptable.
- [ ] Thin cosmetic details are printable.
- [ ] Large flat panels are reinforced.
- [ ] Screw bosses have fillets.
- [ ] Assembly clearances are included.
- [ ] Holes are sized for printing.
- [ ] Paint/post-processing clearance is included.

## Splitting

- [ ] Part fits printer build volume.
- [ ] Split lines follow natural panel lines where possible.
- [ ] Exterior support scars are minimized.
- [ ] Alignment pins or flanges are included.
- [ ] Joints are accessible for glue or screws.

## Export

- [ ] Exported in millimetres.
- [ ] STL or 3MF opened in slicer.
- [ ] Slicer dimensions verified.
- [ ] Curved surfaces are not visibly faceted.
- [ ] Orientation checked.
- [ ] Support placement checked.
- [ ] Slicer project saved.

## Test prints

- [ ] Tolerance coupon printed.
- [ ] Screw boss sample printed.
- [ ] Pin joint sample printed.
- [ ] Wheel/tire sample printed.
- [ ] Body joint sample printed.
- [ ] One body section printed before full model.
