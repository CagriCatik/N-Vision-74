Body & Shell Components¶

Interactive 3D preview of the N-Vision-74 body and shell components.

Overview¶

The body and shell assembly provides the aesthetic and protective outer covering for the vehicle, including the main body shell, bumpers, mirrors, and light housings.

Main Body Shell¶

The primary structure that encloses the vehicle chassis and electronics.

Part Number: 01_Body_Shell_Main.SLDPRT
Material: Polycarbonate (1.5mm)
Weight: ~150g
Dimensions: 280mm L × 150mm W × 100mm H

Features¶

  • Transparent polycarbonate for internal visibility
  • Aerodynamic profile design
  • Mounting posts for secure chassis attachment
  • Integrated cable routing channels
  • Reinforced corner sections for impact resistance

Weight: ~45g

Features¶

  • Wide stance for aerodynamic downforce

Interior Tub¶

The base panel that forms the floor of the vehicle interior.

Part Number: 12_Interior_Tub.SLDPRT
Material: Plastic
Weight: ~60g
Dimensions: 270mm L × 140mm W × 15mm H

Features¶

  • Protective floor for electronics
  • Drainage holes for water escape
  • Cable routing channels
  • Reinforced edges for durability
  • Integrated mounting points

Assembly Notes¶

Installation Sequence¶

  1. Mount interior tub to chassis frame
  2. Install main shell over interior tub
  3. Attach front bumper with fasteners
  4. Attach rear bumper with fasteners
  5. Install mirrors on body sides
  6. Install light housings at front/rear
  7. Verify clearances with wheels turning

Torque Specifications¶

  • M2 fasteners: 0.8-1.0 Nm
  • M2.5 fasteners: 1.2-1.5 Nm
  • M3 fasteners: 1.5-2.0 Nm

Maintenance & Care¶

Cleaning¶

  • Use soft cloth and mild soap
  • Avoid harsh chemicals on polycarbonate
  • Dry thoroughly to prevent water spots
  • Clean mirrors regularly for visibility

Damage Repair¶

  • Minor scratches: use plastic polish
  • Cracks in body: epoxy repair kit
  • Damaged bumpers: replacement is easier
  • Light housing: replace LED if damaged
