Cost Optimization Tips¶

Budget Reduction Strategies¶

  1. Selective 3D Printing ($20-50 savings)
  2. Print non-critical parts yourself

  3. Use cheaper plastic materials

  4. Bulk Fastener Purchases ($10-15 savings)

  5. Buy assorted fastener kits

  6. Lower per-unit costs

  7. Alternative Materials ($30-60 savings)

  8. Use aluminum instead of steel where possible

  9. Standard plastic vs. carbon fiber bracing

  10. Used Components ($50-100 savings)

  11. Source used motors from previous builds
  12. Refurbished electronics

Premium Build Upgrade ($200-300 additional)¶

  • Carbon fiber chassis ($80)
  • High-performance motor ($150)
  • Ball-bearing differentials ($50)
  • Titanium fasteners ($30+)
